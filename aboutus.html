About Us

Meet the Owners

Mr. Doppleganger's family owns several bed and breakfasts in different locations. They are all run seperately by different family members or trusted employees and located around the US -- including Erie, PA, Conneaut Lake, OH, and Put-in-Bay, OH. Mr. Doppleganger is currently 47 and now lives in and runs Home Away From Home B&B. He came to the US as a child with his parents, who opened their first bed and breakfast when he was very young. He runs Home Away From Home B&B with his wife, Frances, and his two daughters, Faith and Emma.

FAQ

Does breakfast cost extra?

No, breakfast is included in the cost of your room.

Are pets allowed?

No, at this time we only allow service animals.

What is the refund/cancellation policy?

If you cancel 24 hours in advance then you get a refund -- otherwise, there is no refund. You can also change the reservation for future use if the room you want is available.
