Meet Your Hosts

Meet your hosts, Mr. and Mrs. Frances Doppleganger. Mr. Doppleganger is 47 years old and came to the United States with his parents, who opened their very first bed and breakfast in 1980. He now owns three other bed and breakfasts in other cities around the country. He currently stays and runs Home Away From Home B&B with his wonderful wife and their two daughters, Faith and Emma.
